Logo

We wanted the logo to be clean but clear and approachable for younger audiences. We settled on using a tree and a shield, as the main action behind the application is to defend/save the forests. The orange of the shield grabs your attention, while the style of the tree matches the style of visuals we use: flat design with 2D shading. The wordmark is a variation of the font Candyful.

Clearance

Acceptable clearance for the logos are listed below. No content may be within the clearance border in order to preserve logo integrity.

Sizing

Logo icon may not be scaled past 50px high for complex, and 25px high for simple. Wordmark may not be smaller than 65px wide.

Do's

                                1. Do place official logo files within your design
                                2. Do make sure the logo has enough contrast with the background
                                3. Do keep other content outside of the clearance area

Don'ts

                                1. Do not outline the logo
                                2. Do not apply non-brand colours
                                3. Do not apply gradients, shadows, or other effects
                                4. Do not rotate
                                5. Do not skew, squeeze, or alter the logo in any way
                                6. Do not scale the logo disporportionately if using both icon 
                                   and wordmark
